New Education Policy 2020

May 15, 2021

Indian Education System Changed by a new policy....The National Education Policy 2020 Was Approved By Union Cabinet of india on 29 July 2020.Our Modi government Union Ministers for Information And Brodcasting(Prakash javadekar) and Human Resource Development Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank MAde Format Of New education policy 2020.The union Cabinet Purpose Was to Improve Our Education System.The Union Minister of Information and Brodcasting prakash javadekar Said on New Education Policy. That the New National Education Policy will Ensure Accessbility and Accountability and Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi Said on New Education.The new education policy Has the Potential to not only Change the Education System but to Also Transform the Entire Nation.

After 34-Years old National Policy of Education Replaced in 2020 New Education Policy.

In 1986,the Government Led By Rajiv Gandhi Introduced a new National Policy on Education but now Modi Government Replaced 1986 Education Policy by 2020 NEP(New Education Policy)

Many Expect Across of India Beleive that the New Education policy is a big change in the Education System for India.

New Framework Design of Education Policy 2020:

1.First the biggest changed is ministry of Human Resources become now Ministry of Education led By Ramesh Pokhriyal.

2.Current 10+2 Structure Replaced by 5+3+3+4 School curriculam with 12 years of Schooling and 3years of Anganwadi pre-schooling.

3.Multi-Stream Now flexibility to choose subjects across Streams No rigid seperation between Academic Stream Extra Curricular,Vocational in schools.

4.Language Policy to Continue With Preference i.e:For Local language medium of instruction till class 8.

5.Teaching upto At least Grade 5 to be in Mother tongue/Regional language.

6.Schools Students to have 10 bag-less Days in a year during Which They are Exposed to a vocation of choice.

6.4-Year Bachelor:4-year multi-disciplinary babchelor's Programme to be prefered.

7.GER in Higher education to be Raised to 50% by 2035, 3.5crore Seats to be added in Higher Education.

8.By 2030,the minimum degree Qualification for teaching will be a 4-year inter grated B.Ed degree.

9.Here Some Key points of biggest Changed in Education Policy.

10.New Education Policy 2020 will Bring 2 crore out of School Children back into the main stream.

Purpose of New Education Policy:

An NEP is a comprehensive Framework Guide the development of education in the country.Government aim to provide infrastructure,support,innovative education.
